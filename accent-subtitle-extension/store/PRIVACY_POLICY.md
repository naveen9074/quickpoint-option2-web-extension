# Privacy Policy for Slang Clarifier

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

Slang Clarifier is a browser extension that provides real-time subtitle overlay with AI-powered slang clarification. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

- **No personal data.** We do not collect names, emails, accounts, or any personally identifiable information.
- **No audio.** Your voice is never recorded or transmitted.

### What Stays on Your Device

- **API Key:** Your Groq API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage. It never leaves your device except when making API requests directly to Groq.
- **Audio:** All audio processing happens locally in your browser using the Web Speech API. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

### What is Sent to Third Parties

- **Groq API:** When slang clarification is enabled, finalized transcription text (not audio) is sent to Groq's API for processing. This is necessary for the AI clarification feature. See [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/).

## Data Storage

- No data is stored on external servers
- No cookies are used
- No analytics or tracking
- No user accounts required

## Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **Microphone:** Required for speech recognition (processed locally)
- **Active Tab:** Required to display the subtitle overlay
- **Storage:** Required to save your API key locally
- **Host Permissions:** Required to run on supported sites (Meet, Zoom, YouTube, Teams, Discord, Twitch)

## Third-Party Services

- **Groq API:** Used for AI-powered slang detection. Only text transcriptions are sent. Review their privacy policy at https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
